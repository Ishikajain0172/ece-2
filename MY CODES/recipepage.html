Rajma Chawal

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making Rajma Chawal ! Rajma Chawal is a well-known dish in India. People specially in north india love Rajma Chawal. It is their daily stable food.It is made of Kidney Beans & Rice.It is simply tasty and easy to make recipe wwith some indian flavour in it.Follow this simple recipe to create delicious rajma chawal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • rajma (kidney beans)
  • rice
  • onions, tomatoes
  • ginger, garlic
  • spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and red chili powder
  • whole spices (bay leaf, cloves)
  • garam masala & fresh coriander

Instructions

  1. Soak & Cook Rajma: Soak rajma overnight, then pressure cook with water & salt until soft.
  2. Make Masala: Saut√© cumin seeds, then onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste, and cook the tomato puree with powdered spices (turmeric, chili, coriander) until oil separates.
  3. Combine: Add boiled rajma (with its water) to the masala, simmer for 15-20 mins, then stir in garam masala and fresh coriander.
  4. Serve: Enjoy with freshly cooked basmati rice (Jeera Rice is great too!) and a dollop of ghee.
